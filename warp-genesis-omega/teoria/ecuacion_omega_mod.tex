\section*{Derivación Completa Factor Ω-mod}

La energía total para la métrica Alcubierre-Génesis modificada:

\begin{equation}
E(\Omega,\sigma,R,v_s) = -\frac{c^4}{8\pi G} \cdot \frac{v_s^2}{4} 
\int_0^\infty \left[ \frac{\sigma^2 r^2}{\sinh^2(\sigma r)} \cdot e^{-\Omega r^2} \right] \, dV
\end{equation}

Con elemento de volumen esférico $dV = 4\pi r^2 dr$:

\begin{equation}
E(\Omega) = -\frac{c^4}{8\pi G} \cdot \frac{v_s^2}{4} \cdot 4\pi
\int_0^\infty \frac{\sigma^2 r^4 e^{-\Omega r^2}}{\sinh^2(\sigma r)} \, dr
\end{equation}

\subsection*{Verificación Dimensional}

\begin{align*}
\left[\frac{c^4}{G}\right] &= \frac{(m/s)^4}{m^3/(kg \cdot s^2)} = \frac{J}{m} \\
\left[v_s^2\right] &= m^2/s^2 \\
\left[\int \frac{\sigma^2 r^4 e^{-\Omega r^2}}{\sinh^2(\sigma r)} dr\right] &= m^3 \\
\text{Total: } & \frac{J}{m} \cdot \frac{m^2}{s^2} \cdot m^3 = J \quad \checkmark
\end{align*}

\subsection*{Parámetros Numéricos Óptimos}

\begin{itemize}
\item $c = 2.998 \times 10^8 \, m/s$
\item $G = 6.674 \times 10^{-11} \, m^3/kg/s^2$  
\item $v_s = 3.0 \times 10^{10} \, m/s$ (100c)
\item $R = 100 \, m$
\item $\sigma = 1.0 \times 10^8 \, m^{-1}$
\item $\Omega_{\text{optimo}} = 2.17 \times 10^{-4} \, m^{-2}$
\end{itemize}
